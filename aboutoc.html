Orange County is home to some of the most beautiful landscapes in the world. Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, and Laguna Beach are all located here and provide amazing backdrops to some of California's best golf courses. Not only does Orange County have beautiful beaches and golf courses, but it is also a booming tech industry and has extremely tasty food. Whether you want to golf, play on the beach, sightsee, eat, or just relax, there's something to do here for everyone! Come and experience Orange County to learn more about why so many people choose here as their vacation destination!
